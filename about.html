Lumen Obscura — Scriptrix Umbrarum.
Corda loquuntur, etiam si mundus non audiat.
I write from the quietest corner of the heart;
where words do not ask to be read, only to be understood.

Lumen Obscura adalah sebuah nama pena dari seseorang yang lebih sering menulis dalam waktu luangnya. Meski sempat menjauh dari dunia tulisan, kini ia kembali, menyusuri jejak jejak sunyi-
yang dulu pernah ia tinggalkan.

Lewat untaian kata, ia mencurahkan hal hal yang tak sempat terucap:
tentang rasa gundah gulana, tetang kehilangan, tetang cinta,
dan tak pula lupa, harapan yang tak selalu pulang.

Scriptum Cordis, Solum Caelis — sub silentio, veritas.
Beneath the silence, the truth.
some of you might ask - what is scriptum cordis, solum caelis?
it translates to a writing of the heart, grounded in the heavens.
the philosophy behind this is that, It represents a space where words are not merely crafted, but born from the depth of feeling; untouched by earthly bounds, floating freely through the skies,
seeking no place but the embrace of the universe.

Every piece written here is a reflection of the silent echoes within,
a whisper from the soul to the stars, a reminder that not all stories are meant to be explained,
some are simply meant to be felt, to drift gently into the vastness of existence,
and find rest not on paper, but in the quiet spaces between hearts.

Singkatnya,
Ini bukan tempat untuk didengar, melainkan untuk dibaca.
Dan mungkin, suatu hari nanti, dipahami.
